Strategy
Overview

Alba Credit Opportunities is a long/short credit strategy investing in liquid corporate and financial credit globally with a European bias. The strategy combines bottom-up fundamental credit analysis with top-down macro positioning to identify asymmetric risk-return opportunities across the capital structure.

Methodology

Investment
Approach

01

Bottom-up fundamental credit research

We conduct deep-dive institutional analysis into complex capital structures, rigorously identifying asymmetric risk-return profiles safely prior to macro sizing.

02

Top-down macro positioning

Translating thematic macroeconomic indicators into strategic sector flows and duration shifts to flawlessly navigate systemic global volatility.

03

Relative value & dislocation opportunities

Exploiting temporary pricing inefficiencies and deeply misunderstood capital tranche dynamics across geographically fragmented European markets.

04

Catalyst-driven investments

Executing high-conviction momentum plays anchored to concrete corporate events, restructuring narratives, and M&A liquidity triggers.

05

Active risk management

Employing aggressive daily portfolio monitoring, downside modeling, and absolute loss barriers mathematically ensuring capital preservation.

06

FX & interest rate hedging

Systematically stripping out unintended cross-border currency drag and interest rate delta via institutional swap and forward overlays.

Portfolio
Construction

The portfolio is built around a concentrated set of high-conviction positions, typically across 25–35 capital structures. The strategy focuses on diversification across issuers, sectors, and risk factors while maintaining flexibility to adjust exposure through derivatives.

Key
Characteristics

Alba Credit Opportunities relies on a concentrated setup to deliver asymmetric risk-return metrics across the capital structure.
