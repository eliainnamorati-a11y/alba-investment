Credit Opportunities

The Alba Credit Opportunities fund is a long/short credit strategy investing in liquid corporate and financial credit globally with a European bias, focusing on high-conviction opportunities across the capital structure. The strategy combines bottom-up fundamental credit analysis with top-down macro positioning and uses derivatives to actively manage interest rate, FX, and credit exposure. The fund aims to generate equity-like returns with contained volatility by exploiting market dislocations, dispersion, and catalyst-driven credit opportunities.
